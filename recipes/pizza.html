This is pizza

Description

Pizza is an Italian, specifically Neapolitan, dish typically consisting of a flat base of leavened wheat-based dough topped with tomato, cheese, and other ingredients, bak ed at a high temperature, traditionally in a wood-fired oven.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough – flour, yeast, water, salt, olive oil
  • Tomato sauce – made from tomatoes, garlic, herbs like oregano/basil
  • Cheese – usually mozzarella.
  • Toppings – like onions, capsicum, olives, mushrooms, pepperoni, etc.
  • Seasonings – oregano, chili flakes, black pepper.

Steps to make Pizza

  1. Prepare ingredients – get pizza dough, tomato sauce, cheese, and your favorite toppings ready
  2. Preheat oven – usually around 220°C (425°F).
  3. Assemble pizza – roll out dough, spread tomato sauce, sprinkle cheese, and add toppings
  4. Bake – place pizza in the oven for 12–15 minutes or until crust is golden and cheese melts
  5. Serve – slice and enjoy hot!
