Margherita Pizza

Pizza is an Italian, specifically Neapolitan, dish typically consisting of a flat base of leavened wheat-based dough topped with tomato, cheese, and other ingredients, baked at a high temperature, traditionally in a wood-fired oven.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough – flour, yeast, water, salt, olive oil
  • Tomato sauce – tomatoes, garlic, oregano/basil
  • Cheese – usually mozzarella
  • Toppings – onions, capsicum, olives, mushrooms, pepperoni, etc.
  • Seasonings – oregano, chili flakes, black pepper

Steps to Make Pizza

  1. Prepare ingredients – get pizza dough, tomato sauce, cheese, and toppings ready.
  2. Preheat oven – usually around 220°C (425°F).
  3. Assemble pizza – roll out dough, spread sauce, sprinkle cheese, add toppings.
  4. Bake – 12–15 minutes until crust is golden and cheese melts.
  5. Serve – slice and enjoy hot!
