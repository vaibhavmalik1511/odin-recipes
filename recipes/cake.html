This is cake

Description

A layered pound cake with a golden-brown crust, neatly sliced and arranged on a white plate, set on a wooden table

Ingredients

  • flour
  • sugar
  • eggs
  • fat (such as butter, oil, or margarine)
  • a liquid
  • a leavening agent

Steps to make delicious cake

  1. Prepare – gather ingredients (flour, sugar, eggs, butter, baking powder) and preheat oven.
  2. Mix – cream butter & sugar, add eggs, then fold in flour and baking powder.
  3. Pour – transfer batter into a greased baking tin.
  4. Bake – place in oven, bake until golden and a toothpick comes out clean
  5. Cool & Decorate – let it cool, then frost or serve as is
