Beef Patty burger

Description

A freshly prepared burger with a sesame seed bun, lettuce, tomato, cheese, and a beef patty, placed on a wooden table with a rustic background

Ingredients

  • sesame seed bun
  • lettuce
  • tomato
  • cheese
  • beef patty

Steps to make a beef patty burger

  1. Prepare the ingredients – wash lettuce, slice tomato, get cheese slices, and sesame seed buns ready
  2. Cook the beef patty – season with salt & pepper, then grill or pan-fry until cooked through.
  3. Toast the buns – lightly toast the sesame seed buns on a pan or grill for extra flavor.
  4. Assemble the burger – place lettuce and tomato on the bottom bun, add the hot beef patty, then top with cheese.
  5. Finish & serve – add sauces (ketchup, mayo, etc.), put on the top bun, and enjoy!
